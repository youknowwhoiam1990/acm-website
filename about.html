ABOUT ACM

The Association for Computing Machinery (ACM) is an international learning society for computing. It was founded in 1947, and is the world's largest scientific and educational computing society. The ACM is a non-profit professional membership group. The ACM is an umbrella organization for academic and scholarly interests in computer science. Its motto is "Advancing Computing as a Science & Profession". It is a platform where all the computing researchers, educators, professionals work together for the advancement of the society. ACM is delighted to welcome the new ideas, discoveries and inventions from all around the globe to the ACM family. Take initiative and start an ACM Student Chapter at your college today!

ACM offers access to following services:
  1. e-Books
  2. Videos
  3. Lectures and panel discussions
  4. Online courses
  5. Digital library
  6. Live seminars
  7. Certification training
